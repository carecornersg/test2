This section explains the reports available in CAS and how to use them.

## Overview

Reports help Call Centre Staff and AAYS Management track performance, coverage gaps, and operational metrics. Most reports support filtering and export.

**Who can access reports (typical permission model):**

- **Call Centre Staff**: Can access **Call Centre Operational Reports** (day-to-day operational tracking)
- **AAYS Manager**: Can access **all report categories** (operational + management/KPI dashboards)
- **System Administrator**: Can access **all report categories** (and manages system configuration)

---

## How to Run a Report (Step-by-step)

### Step 1: Open Reporting

In the left-hand side menu, open **Reports**.

### Step 2: Select a Report Tab

Each report is shown as a **tab**. Click the tab you want to view.

### Step 3: Filter by Date Range

Select the **date range** you want to report on.

### Step 5: Export

Click **Export** (CSV/Excel) to download the filtered results.

## Report Categories (Summary)

Below is a simplified summary of the reporting modules described in the functional specification.

### Call Centre Operational Reports

Used for day-to-day operational tracking.

- Total Cases Created
- Cases Completed
- Cases Cancelled
- Activation calls (cases broadcasted)
- Manpower deployed (total AAs activated)
- Case recalls/edits (edits after broadcast)
- Mean number of activations
- Median number of activations

### AAYS Management Reports

Used for program KPIs and oversight.

- No. of AA/staff activations
- No. of regular AAs (4+ monthly cases)
- No. of AA hours (total service hours)
- No. of beneficiaries served (youth assisted count)
- No. of activations by type of offense
- No. of activations by region
- No. of activations from 2300hrs to 0700hrs
- No. of activations that are home visits
- No. of activations that are not Divisions/NPCs
- No. of activations by agency
- No. of AAs activated by region

### Notification Delivery Reports

Used to troubleshoot push notification delivery.

- Sent / delivered / viewed counts
- Failed deliveries

### AA Engagement Reports

Used to track response behaviour.

- Response rates, acceptance/rejection rates
- Response time trends

- Payable vs not payable documentation counts

### Last-minute Staff Activations

Used to track urgent escalation cases.

- Staff-only broadcasts (B3) volume
- Last-minute triggers
- Breakdown by region/time

### MHA Monthly Report

Used for monthly submission.

- Agency/region summaries
- Case outcomes and required reporting fields

### Volunteer Feedback / Wellbeing
